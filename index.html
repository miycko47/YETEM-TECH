ABOUT US

Ethiopia is one of the rapidly growing nations in the world. And with its growth, the country's population increases drastically. The capital Addis Ababa is a vibrant city with its borders widening due to the high rate of population rise & urbanization. Because of the high rate of urbanization, the overall connection & relationship between urban zones have been compromised. Specifically, the proximity between residential, commercial & industrial zones, which is the major reason for many problems that we face in our day to day life like Inadequate facilities, shortage of infrastructures like roads, water, electricity, transportation & many necessities. Even though the economy is growing in every aspect, there are still some gaps yet to be filled. As a young, energetic & innovative entrepreneurs we feel obligated to identify the missing gaps and fill in as best we can, thereby enhancing the living condition of the society, creating multiple job opportunities, cultivating a healthy competition among businesses and competitors, and in general carving a better environment for the many future generations to come.
